# Product Overview

CryptoScore is a professional Web2 prediction market application that allows users to create, join, and resolve prediction markets. The platform has been migrated from Solana blockchain to a Supabase-powered backend while maintaining EVM wallet integration through Crossmint.

## Core Features

- **Market Trading**: Create, join, and resolve prediction markets with real-time updates
- **Portfolio Dashboard**: Track performance, P&L, win rates, and transaction history
- **Social Authentication**: Crossmint integration supporting Google, Twitter, Farcaster, and Email login with EVM wallet creation
- **Real-time Updates**: Live market data via Supabase subscriptions
- **Theme System**: 6 professional theme presets (Dark Terminal, Ocean Blue, Forest Green, Sunset Orange, Purple Haze, Light Mode)
- **PWA Support**: Installable app with offline capability
- **Full Accessibility**: WCAG AA compliant with keyboard navigation

## Key Business Logic

- Platform fee: 5% on market transactions
- Market duration: 1 hour minimum, 30 days maximum
- EVM wallet addresses stored for future MNEE token integration
- Real-time market updates with configurable rate limiting
- Automated market resolution and reward distribution

## Target Users

Professional traders and prediction market participants who value:
- Clean, accessible UI with multiple theme options
- Real-time data and notifications
- Portfolio tracking and analytics
- Social login convenience with crypto wallet benefits
